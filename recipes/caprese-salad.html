Caprese Salad

Description

A celebration of simplicity and freshness, Caprese Salad is a beloved Italian dish that pays homage to the sun-ripened flavors of the Mediterranean. Named after the island of Capri, this salad features ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves, drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze. Each ingredient shines in its own right, with the juicy tomatoes providing a burst of sweetness, the velvety mozzarella lending a creamy texture, and the aromatic basil infusing the dish with a refreshing herbal note. The marriage of flavors and textures creates a harmonious balance that captures the essence of Italian cuisine. Whether enjoyed as a light appetizer, a side dish, or a standalone meal, Caprese Salad is a timeless classic that embodies the spirit of la dolce vita./p>

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 1 ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange alternating slices of tomatoes and mozzarella cheese on a serving platter.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves in between the tomato and cheese slices.
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze over the top.
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, drain it, reserving some of the cooking water.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste..
  6. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
