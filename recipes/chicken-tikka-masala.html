Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Hailing from the heart of Italy, Spaghetti Carbonara is a quintessential pasta dish loved for its simplicity and rich flavors. Originating from Rome, this dish traditionally consists of spaghetti pasta coated in a velvety sauce made from eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, black pepper, and crispy pancetta or bacon. The hot pasta cooks the eggs just enough to create a creamy texture without scrambling them. The result is a harmonious blend of salty, smoky, and creamy flavors that dance on the palate with each bite. Spaghetti Carbonara embodies the essence of Italian comfort food, offering a satisfying and indulgent dining experience..

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons tikka masala spice blend
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces, yogurt, and tikka masala spice blend. Marinate for at least 1 hour, or preferably overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Stir in the diced tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring until heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving. Serve with rice or naan bread.
