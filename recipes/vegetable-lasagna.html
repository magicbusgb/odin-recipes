Vegetable Lasagna

Description

A comforting and hearty dish, Vegetable Lasagna is a vegetarian twist on the classic Italian favorite, showcasing layers of tender lasagna noodles, creamy ricotta cheese, and a medley of sautéed vegetables bathed in marinara sauce. This wholesome and satisfying dish is a celebration of seasonal produce, with ingredients such as spinach, mushrooms, zucchini, and bell peppers adding color, flavor, and nutritional value. Topped with a blanket of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, each slice of Vegetable Lasagna is a symphony of flavors and textures, offering a taste of home-cooked comfort with every bite. Whether served as a main course for a family dinner or as the centerpiece of a festive gathering, Vegetable Lasagna is a timeless classic that delights the senses and warms the soul.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cupsricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as spinach, mushrooms, zucchini, and bell peppers), chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté the minced garlic in a bit of olive oil until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook until tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with half of the shredded mozzarella and half of the grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Arrange 3 cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by half of the cooked vegetables.
  8. Repeat the layers with marinara sauce, noodles, remaining ricotta cheese mixture, and remaining cooked vegetables.
  9. Top with the remaining marinara sauce, shredded mozzarella, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Enjoy!
